under the aegis of the World Economic Forum's AIYC

Who are we?

The World Economic Forum has developed the global AI Youth Council, which brings together youth from across the world to discuss Artificial Intelligence ethics and policy. AIYC India takes this forward, led by the India representative, as the independant national chapter of the World Economic Forum’s global council.

Educate

Develop frameworks and toolkits to educate and inspire children, adolescents, parents and guardians around the responsible use of AI.

Empower

Empower children and young people with AI skills to create their own technology to improve the state of the world with an emphasis on underrepresented voices.

Protect

Protect and expand children’s human rights and civil liberties when encountering AI in their homes, schools and public places. 

Our Objective

As the first national chapter globally

Through sub committees for responsible AI policy, onboarding youth, mentor outreach and more, AIYC India leads with the following objectives -

Recruiting and coordinating with members through outreach to school technology clubs (creating school/college chapters of AIYC India)

Conducting collaborative events with certain or all council members to discuss the responsible use and education of Artificial Intelligence

Connecting students with global experts in supporting technical AI partner projects and awareness initiatives.

Connecting students, schools, and universities across the country


AIYC India is extending its reach by connecting with tech clubs across India and establishing interconnected chapters to provide students with more interesting, practical and responsible AI learning opportunities!
